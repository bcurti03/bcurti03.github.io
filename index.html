Goals

Seek information pertaining to given problems and identify the causes, both independently & timely

By the end of the term I was able to quickly identify and fix issues, partly due to dealing with the similar issues earier in the term, such as troubleshooting a office wide adobe acrobat crashing issue.

Independentally devise and research fixes to software and harware issues

It came to the point where I was able to fix multiple issues within an hour or two without needing to consult my supervisor (in part due to expereince with other similar issues).

Understand the numerous processes and solutions that assist in performing my day-to-day activities

An example that reflects this goal, was when I was tasked with mentoring another employee with the technical process involved in cloning and creating a split disk format of a VM. Thanks to my understanding of the process, I was able to succesfully teach another employee a technical skill important to a project.

Adhear to the stated due dates on office tasks, and complete tasks without set dates in order of importance

Tasks were generally completed prior on on their due dates but the realities of the postition resulted in certain lower priority tasks being delayed to focus on higher priority tasks.

Convey my points in a clear and concise manner, for a diverse range of readers

Two sentences was the amount I was able to hone the large majority of my explanations to and this was the same between both technical and non-technical employees.

During my time at Eramosa Engineering I provided in-office IT support for employees.

My day-to-day tasks involved cloning and modifying the format of Virtual Machines, troubleshooting everyday software and hardware issues, rolling out new laptops and replacement laptops, as well as work phones, writing documentation for in-house use, and managing user permission's through Azure AD, Intune, and Windows Server. Most of these tasks required a certain depth of understanding with the Windows OS and it's system application, in tandem with system application by manufacturer(mainly Dell, Lenovo and HP)
